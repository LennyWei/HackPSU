Gesture AI

Train, Bind, Predict. GestureAI allows you to use your webcam, a chronically underused part of every computer, to bind hand gestures to any keyboard & mouse action. Train - This is where you will need to train the model. Test - In the Test tab you can see what gestures are being recognized. Data Collection - Here you can train yout OWN model! simply put up a gesture and fill in the frames and delay then press collect! Gestures - You can bind your gestures to your keyboard in this tab. You will also be able to delete your gestures here.
